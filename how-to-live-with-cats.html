How to live with Cats

It’s not that living with cats is difficult, it’s just that they can suddenly seem to take over our lives. After adding a cat to your home, you soon realize that you really belong to the cat – not the other way around. Our cats are the head of the household! Whether you’re struggling to adapt to your new schedule or just finally realizing you want to make things a little lighter on yourself, these tips will make living with a cat so much easier. Her I left you a list of nine thing you have to keep in mind:

  1. Trim Nails Regularly
  2. Offer a Safe Place
  3. Get a Spray Bottle
  4. Grow Your Own Catnip
  5. Accept What You Can’t Change
  6. Make Your Own Cat Hammock
  7. Clean Out Your Closet and Make a Cat Bed
  8. User a Carrier As a Bed
  9. Put Bird Feeders Outside Windows
